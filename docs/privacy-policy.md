# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 29, 2026

## Overview

Kairos is a personal habit tracking application. The hosted version of this app is provided to a closed group of invited users only and is not open to the general public. This policy explains what data the app stores, where it is stored, and how it is used.

## Data We Collect

### Account Information

When you sign in, Kairos uses **Firebase Authentication** (provided by Google) to create and manage your account. This processes your email address and password. Firebase Authentication is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Habit Data

Your habits, completions, streaks, routines, and related data are stored in **Cloud Firestore** (provided by Google). Your data is:

- Isolated to your account -- no other user can access your data
- Synced across your own devices in real time
- Stored within the Kairos Firebase project, which is administered by the developer

We do not read, analyze, sell, or share your habit data with any third party for any purpose.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect analytics or usage statistics
- We do not collect device identifiers
- We do not use advertising SDKs
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

Kairos uses the following Google services, each subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy):

| Service                 | Purpose                             |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Firebase Authentication | User sign-in and account management |
| Cloud Firestore         | Habit data storage and sync         |

No other third-party SDKs or services are used.

## Self-Hosting

Kairos is open source. If you build the app from source using your own Firebase project credentials, your data is stored entirely within your own Firebase account and is never accessible to the developer. See the repository for self-hosting instructions.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your habit data is retained until you delete your account. To delete your data:

1. Delete your account from within the app (Settings), or
2. Contact us at the address below and we will delete your data within 30 days

## Children's Privacy

Kairos is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
